Micro Project Ideas

Depression

System: This will show the experience of shrinking smaller in a world wear everything goes round.

Interaction: As you click the black circle will get smaller and smaller, there will be lots of happy colorful dots bouncing around.

Intended feeling: sadness and hopeless


Love

System: This will show how love gives you the space to grow and evolve into a more beautiful version of yourself alongside someone else.

Interaction: 2 shapes are in some constraints (like in their own bubble or universe) but when the constraints bump into each other, the shapes come together. The shapes grow and grow and turn different colors each time you click.

Intended feeling: hope, beauty, love


Adventure

System: The experience of growing and changing through travel and seeing the world.

Interaction: Theres a big globe and as you click in certain places on the globe, a fact pops up, and the more facts you learn the bigger the dot grows, and the circle eventually changes into a big bright star over time.

Intended feeling: optimism, knowledge, appreciation
