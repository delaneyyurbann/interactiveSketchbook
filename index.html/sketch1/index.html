This sketch is slowwwwwwww. When you let your cursor hover over the sage green plane the circle will drift back and forth in a slow manner. I chose sage green as the background and a neutral gray for the circle because these colors are very calming to me. I wanted this to not only be slow but also relaxed, which I think is achieved by the color palette and slow rocking of the dot. =]
